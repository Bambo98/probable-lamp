What are Aliens by Bambo?

Aliens by Bambo are a collection of 10,000 aliens all unique to one and other with over 100 traits. Aliens by Bambo live on the polygon blockchain to reduce the gas fees for everyone and to make them more accessible for everyone in the nft space. This is our teams first big project and we hope to create a community around the aliens and give back to the early supporters by making the first aliens the cheapest and making the early giveaways limited.



What is the roadmap?

We are planning for the first drop to include 100 aliens at 0.001 ETH each. Depending on when they sell there will be a giveaway of $100 decided and voted on by the community and 1 completely unique alien with new traits to seperate winners. The giveaway will either take place 2 weeks after release or when the 100 sells if they are not gone after the first 2 weeks.

Every 2 weeks after that giveaway 100 more will drop but at 0.0005 ETH more than before repeating the process. An extra $50 onto the giveaway and 1 unique alien!

The giveaways will be decided by your aliens number. A random number will be generated and automatically airdropped to the winner. Therefore the more you own the more chances you have!
